Iconic Moments

Sextuple Glory - 2009

In 2009, under Pep Guardiola, FC Barcelona made history by winning all six major trophies in a single year — La Liga, Copa del Rey, UEFA Champions League, Spanish Super Cup, UEFA Super Cup, and FIFA Club World Cup.

This Sextuple remains one of the most dominant campaigns in football history.
